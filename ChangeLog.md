# Change log for vim-ormolu

## 0.4.0

* Expose OrmoluBlock
* Disable formatting globally with `g:ormolu_disable`.

## 0.3.0

* Expose `RunOrmolu()`.
* Expose `DisableOrmolu()`.
* Expose `ToggleOrmolu()`.

## 0.2.0

* Disable formatting on specific buffers with `b:ormolu_disable`.

## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
